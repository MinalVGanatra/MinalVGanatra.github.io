Education is one of the most effective ways to confront poverty. Our mission is to create a global connection between contributors like yourself and those with unequal access, providing them the means to claim an education.

FEDU - Fund Education

It stands for a deep belief that socio-economic circumstances should never act as a barrier to receiving education. FEDU means mobilizing our collective resources to connect those with unequal access to the educational needs that will directly impact their quality of life.

FEDU STORY

  • CSR

In April of 2009, Chet was volunteering at a religious event for JCNC when he was approached by a gentleman raising money for a temple. Chet asked the man if the money would go directly to helping an individual or cause and the reply was "no." That was the moment a vision was created. Chet wanted to find a way to channel money directly to those in need. He began funding an underprivileged student named Sagar, helping him receive technical vocational training.

A few months after Chet funded Sagar's training, Sagar graduated, began working and eventually returned the money loaned to Chet with a simple message: "Take this money back and use it to help others like me." From then on, Chet was driven to create a scaleable ecosystem for funding education that could go on to help many others like Sagar.

Why FEDU?

We develop trust

We do our best to honor your contributions by making the giving process as reliable and transparent as possible. To do so, we vet the community partners we collaborate with, and work through them to identify deserving, high-potential individuals who need funding. In addition, we deposit your contributions directly with our partners, not the beneficiaries themselves. This allows us to collect reports from our partners, indicating that funds were used properly.

We keep you connected

We do our best to deliver real change for your dollar. We follow-up on the impact of your contribution so you’re never giving into a void. By collaborating with community partners already working on the ground, we’re able to close the feedback loop to track how students are benefiting from the funding they received and report that change back to you.

We facilitate giving end to end

We see giving as a process, not a transaction. This means not only connecting you to trusted nonprofits and amazing students, but allowing you to feel involved in their education and confident in your impact. For businesses, we offer customized matching with partners who support your corporate mission and help with communicating your impact via PR efforts, marketing materials and customized infographics.
