WELCOME TO FEDU

Education is one of the most effective ways to confront poverty. Our mission is to create a global connection between contributors like yourself and those with unequal access, providing them the means to claim an education.

FEDU - Fund Education

It stands for a deep belief that socio-economic circumstances should never act as a barrier to receiving education. FEDU means mobilizing our collective resources to connect those with unequal access to the educational needs that will directly impact their quality of life.

In April of 2009, Chet was volunteering at a religious event for JCNC when he was approached by a gentleman raising money for a temple. Chet asked the man if the money would go directly to helping an individual or cause and the reply was "no." That was the moment a vision was created. Chet wanted to find a way to channel money directly to those in need. He began funding an underprivileged student named Sagar, helping him receive technical vocational training.

A few months after Chet funded Sagar's training, Sagar graduated, began working and eventually returned the money loaned to Chet with a simple message: "Take this money back and use it to help others like me." From then on, Chet was driven to create a scaleable ecosystem for funding education that could go on to help many others like Sagar.
